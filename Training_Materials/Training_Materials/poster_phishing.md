 Phishing Awareness Poster

This poster teaches healthcare staff how to quickly identify and report phishing attempts that target patient data, clinical systems, and internal accounts.

 Key Message
Phishing is the #1 cause of healthcare data breaches — stay alert.

 Red Flags to Watch For
- Unexpected emails asking you to click a link
- Urgent or threatening language (“Your account will be disabled”)
- Misspelled sender addresses
- Attachments you weren’t expecting
- Links that look suspicious or unfamiliar
- Requests for passwords or MFA codes

 Visual Tagline
**“Think Before You Click — Protect Patient Information.”**

 Intended Use
This poster is ideal for:
- Nursing stations  
- Staff lounges  
- Clinical workrooms  
- Email awareness campaigns  
- Monthly security themes

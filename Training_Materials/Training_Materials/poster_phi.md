 PHI Protection Awareness Poster

This poster reminds healthcare staff of their responsibility to protect Personal Health Information (PHI) at all times, both digitally and physically.

 Key Message
PHI is sensitive, regulated, and must be protected to maintain patient trust and comply with healthcare privacy laws.

 PHI Protection Rules
- Access PHI only when required for your job
- Never share patient information through email or messaging apps
- Lock your screen when stepping away
- Dispose of printed PHI in secure shredding bins
- Avoid discussing patient details in public or open areas
- Report any suspected privacy incident immediately

 Visual Tagline
**“Protect PHI — Protect Patients.”**

 Intended Use
This poster is ideal for:
- Clinical units  
- Registration desks  
- Staff training rooms  
- Privacy awareness campaigns  
- Compliance onboarding

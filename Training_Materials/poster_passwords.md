 Password Security Awareness Poster

This poster provides simple, memorable guidance to help healthcare staff create strong, secure passwords and reduce the risk of unauthorized access.

 Key Message
Strong passwords protect patient data, clinical systems, and your own accounts.

 Core Principles
- Use at least 12 characters
- Combine uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
- Avoid patient names, birthdays, or common words
- Never reuse passwords across systems
- Enable multi‑factor authentication (MFA) wherever possible

 Visual Tagline
**“Strong Passwords Save Lives — Protect Patient Data.”**

 Intended Use
This poster is designed for:
- Staff lounges  
- Nursing stations  
- Break rooms  
- Onboarding materials  
- Monthly awareness campaigns

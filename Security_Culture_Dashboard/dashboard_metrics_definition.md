 Dashboard Metrics Definition

This document defines each metric used in the Security Culture Power BI Dashboard to ensure clarity, consistency, and accurate interpretation by leadership and security teams.

 Phishing Simulation Failure Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of employees who clicked a phishing link or opened a malicious attachment during simulations.  
**Formula:** (Number of failures ÷ Total participants) × 100  
**Purpose:** Measures vulnerability to phishing attacks.

 Phishing Reporting Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of employees who correctly reported a phishing simulation.  
**Formula:** (Number of reports ÷ Total participants) × 100  
**Purpose:** Indicates awareness and proactive security behavior.

 Training Completion Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of staff who completed mandatory security awareness training.  
**Formula:** (Completed trainings ÷ Assigned trainings) × 100  
**Purpose:** Tracks compliance and engagement.

 Password Hygiene Compliance
**Definition:** Percentage of users following password best practices (length, complexity, no reuse).  
**Data Source:** Identity and access management logs.  
**Purpose:** Measures adherence to secure authentication practices.

 MFA Adoption Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of accounts protected by multi‑factor authentication.  
**Formula:** (MFA‑enabled accounts ÷ Total accounts) × 100  
**Purpose:** Indicates resilience against credential compromise.

 Incident Response Time
**Definition:** Average time taken to detect, contain, and resolve human‑factor security incidents.  
**Purpose:** Measures operational efficiency and readiness.

 Repeat Offender Rate
**Definition:** Percentage of employees who repeatedly fail phishing simulations.  
**Purpose:** Identifies individuals or departments needing targeted training.

 Department Risk Score
**Definition:** Weighted score combining phishing failures, reporting rate, training completion, and incident history.  
**Purpose:** Highlights high‑risk areas requiring additional support.

 Awareness Engagement Score
**Definition:** Measures participation in monthly campaigns (poster views, email opens, quiz completions).  
**Purpose:** Tracks cultural engagement and campaign effectiveness.

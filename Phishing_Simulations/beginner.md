  Beginner Phishing Simulation Scenario

This beginner-level phishing simulation is designed to introduce healthcare staff to common phishing patterns in a safe, controlled environment.

  Objective
Help employees recognize basic phishing red flags such as:
- Suspicious sender addresses
- Unexpected attachments
- Urgent or threatening language
- Requests for login credentials

  Scenario Summary
A fake email pretending to be from the IT department asks users to “verify their account” by clicking a link.

  Expected Behavior
- Do NOT click the link
- Report the email using the organization’s phishing reporting process

  Learning Outcome
Employees learn to pause, inspect, and report suspicious emails instead of reacting quickly.

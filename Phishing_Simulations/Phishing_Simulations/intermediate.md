Intermediate Phishing Simulation Scenario

This intermediate-level phishing simulation introduces more realistic and sophisticated phishing patterns commonly seen in healthcare environments.

Objective
Help employees identify:
- Spoofed internal email addresses
- Fake vendor notifications
- Emails with near‑perfect grammar
- Links that redirect to credential‑harvesting pages

Scenario Summary
A fake email appears to come from a known healthcare vendor, notifying staff of an “updated invoice” or “policy change.” The link leads to a realistic but fake login page.

Expected Behavior
- Do NOT enter credentials
- Verify the request through official channels
- Report the email using the organization’s phishing reporting process

Learning Outcome
Employees learn to verify sender identity and inspect URLs before interacting with emails that appear legitimate.

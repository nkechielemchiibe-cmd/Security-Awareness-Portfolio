 Advanced Phishing Simulation Scenario

This advanced-level phishing simulation replicates highly targeted attacks used against healthcare organizations, including spear‑phishing and credential‑harvesting campaigns.

 Objective
Help employees identify:
- Highly personalized phishing emails
- Emails referencing real internal projects or colleagues
- Sophisticated spoofed domains
- Malicious attachments disguised as clinical documents

 Scenario Summary
A fake email appears to come from a department leader or physician, referencing a real project or patient case. It includes an attachment labeled “Updated Patient Report” that contains malware.

 Expected Behavior
- Do NOT open the attachment
- Verify the request directly with the sender
- Report the email immediately

 Learning Outcome
Employees learn to recognize targeted attacks that rely on personalization, urgency, and insider knowledge.
